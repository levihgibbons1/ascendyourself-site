Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Ascend AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Ascend AI mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Email address when you create an account.
  • Photos: Selfies you upload for facial analysis. These are processed by our AI service and stored securely.
  • Usage data: App activity such as task completions, streak data, and analysis history.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for support purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the facial analysis and personalized routine features.
  • To track your progress and maintain your streak history.
  • To send push notifications (only with your permission) about daily tasks and reminders.
  • To process subscription payments via RevenueCat.
  • To respond to support requests.

Photos and AI Processing

Photos you upload are sent to OpenAI's API for facial analysis. By uploading a photo, you consent to this processing. We do not sell, license, or share your photos with any third party beyond what is necessary to provide the service. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Settings screen.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication.
  • OpenAI: AI-powered facial analysis.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and in-app purchases.
  • Apple: App distribution, push notifications, and StoreKit payments.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data and uploaded photos are permanently deleted within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Ascend AI is not intended for users under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by deleting your account in Settings or contacting us at the email below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.
